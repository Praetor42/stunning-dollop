Faction Representatives

Sildar Hallwinter
Was bodyguard and friend to Gundren Rockseeker. A retired member of the griffon cavalry of Waterdeep, he is the local Lord's Alliance contact.
Sister Grael
Is young zealous elf who is an acolyte of Tymore, the goddess of luck. She is also the local contact for The Harper's
Daran Edermath
He is a retired adventurer who lives in a tidy little cottage beside an apple orchard. He served as a marshal for many years. He is retired and serves as the local contact for The Order of the Gauntlet
Reidoth
A druid near Thundertree who is the local representative of the The Emerald Enclave
Halia Thornton
Runs the Miner's exchange in Phandalin. She is the local Zhentarim contact.

Friendlies

Droop
A goblin rescued by the party underneath Tresendar Manor. Weak hearted and weak bodied, he was bullied by his goblinoid peers. He earned his name from a birth defect that makes part of his face droop.
Hamun Kost
A Thayan wizard who is in the area to do an excavation of Old Owl Well. A wizard of the necromancy school, he uses ethically sourced bodies as his zombie workforce. He has recently had problems with the orcs at Wyvren Tor.
