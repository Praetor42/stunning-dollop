Faction Representatives

Sildar Hallwinter
Was bodyguard and friend to Gundren Rockseeker. A retired member of the griffon cavalry of Waterdeep, he is the local Lord's Alliance contact.
Sister Grael
Is young zealous elf who is an acolyte of Tymore, the goddess of luck. She is also the local contact for The Harper's
Daran Edermath
He is a retired adventurer who lives in a tidy little cottage beside an apple orchard. He served as a marshal for many years. He is retired and serves as the local contact for The Order of the Gauntlet
Reidoth
A druid near Thundertree who is the local representative of the The Emerald Enclave
Halia Thornton

Friendlies

Droop
A goblin rescued by the party underneath Tresendar Manor. Weak hearted and weak bodied, he was bullied by his goblinoid peers. He earned his name from a birth defect that makes part of his face droop.
Hamun Kost
A Thayan wizard who is in the area to do an excavation of Old Owl Well. A wizard of the necromancy school, he uses ethically sourced bodies as his zombie workforce. He has recently had problems with the orcs at Wyvren Tor.
Lady Vesa Wernheardt
A well known paladin of Tyr, god of justice, she is currently in Phandalin acting as a representative of Neverwinter and the factions. Although she is a member of the Lord's Alliance she is trusted enough to be an impartial arbiter, due to her oath of honesty and integrity. She belongs to a small sect known as The Sisters of Mercy, a branch of Tyr worshippers that believe in moderating pure unrelenting justice with mercy and compassion. A powerful and traveled adventurer, She took the position in Phandalin as a way of taking a break. Her goal is the safety of the town, and as such will not leave to deal with problems she believes to be within the capabilities of the Grasping Otters.

Enemies

The Black Spider
An unknown figure who has been pulling the strings behind Gundren's kidnapping and the Redbrand's activities. He recently murdered Droop, nailing a note to his forhead warning the party to return to Neverwinter. Unbeknownest to him this only served to enrage and motivate the party.
Condon
A figure from Siffress's past, convinced she is behind his beloved's disapearence. Siffress convinced his fiancee to find her own path in life, which lead to her leaving to find herself, only leaving a simple note to Condon. This led him to be suspicous of Siffress, her being a smuggler she could have easily kidnapped his fiancee. He found another lover who also disappeared and was also an associate of Siffress. This has lead him to be convinced that she is a malevolent kidnapper and he is using all of his connections as an entertainer to spread the word.
