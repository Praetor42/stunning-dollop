Main Quest

The major focus of the group is the locating and rescuing of their friend and benefactor, Gundren Rockseeker from the Cragmaw Goblins.

Side Quests

Finding Iarno
Iarno is a short dark-bearded human wizard in his thirties. He is a scout for the Lord's Alliance who went missing in the area.
Orc Trouble
Travelers have reported trouble with a band of orcs near Wyvern Tor.
Redbrand Rumble
The Redbrands have been harassing the town of Phandalin, handle them one way or another.
Eliminating/Capturing Glasstaff
You have been tasked by several people to remove Glasstaff, the leader of the Redbrands, from the situation.
Banshee's Bargain
You are asked to persuade a banshee Agatha to answer a question about a spellbook.
Old Owl Trouble
There are tales of undead near Old Owl Well from local prospectors. Investigate the situation.
Lost Heirloom
There may be a valuable emerald necklace hidden in the old apothecary's shop in Thundertree. You are free to have it, should you survive the retrival.
Reidoth, local druid
You heard talk that there is a druid around Thundertree, he may know more about locations in the area, specifically Cragmaw Castle.
