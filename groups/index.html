Redbrands

The Redbrands mostly consisted of humans, male and female, although a handful of bugbears were also in the bandit group. Each Redbrand wore a simple, dirty scarlet cloak. These cloaks were recognized by the undead in their hideout, Tresendar Manor, and stopped them from attacking the bandits. They were led by a mysterious wizard who they called Glasstaff.

Goblins of Cragmaw

The group behind the kidnapping of Gundren rockseeker and Sildar Hallwinter. Their base of operations is Cragmaw Castle. It's specific location is unknown , the only info is that it is to the north of Phandalin. They seem to be well organized and invested in discovering Wave Echo Cave. So far they are made up of Bugbears and Goblins.
